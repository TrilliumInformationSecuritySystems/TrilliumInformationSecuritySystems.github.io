About TISS

As the regional leader in cybersecurity, Trillium Information Security Systems has been offering top of the line cybersecurity services, solutions and training to its customers since 2005. We possess the strongest, most qualified and constantly evolving resource and knowledge base in the entire region. Our unique skillsets have enabled us to easily build long-term relationships with leading international vendor organizations and strategic thought leaders from across the globe.

All of this starts with a culture that believes in giving you the training, opportunities and creative freedom to make things better. Whenever you join, however long you stay, the exceptional TISS experience lasts a lifetime.

About Company

With our pedigree in Cybersecurity spanning nearly two decades, we have cemented our position as the cybersecurity trailblazers in the region. Our unique skillset, combined with our ever-evolving resource and knowledge base, has helped us foster deep-rooted relationship with leading organizations globally.

TISS Future Leaders Program

For several years, TISS Future Leaders Program has been preparing the next generation of cybersecurity and business leaders by providing a platform to those brilliant and career-oriented graduates who want to put their academic achievements and intellect to use.The program enables them to apply concepts, protocols and tools acquired through coursework in the real world by working side by side with experts.

At TISS we take pride in promoting a culture full of development opportunities and we are committed to building a diverse pool of future leaders and bridging the skill gap. TISS Future Leaders Program blends your unique qualities with a tailored development approach, providing you with the opportunity to continuously learn and grow.

We are committed to hiring and developing the most passionate people, it is our ambition to reduce the skill shortage by strengthening the Industry - Academia linkage and nurturing fresh graduates and giving them an opportunity to become the leaders of tomorrow.
