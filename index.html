About Us

Region’s only full spectrum
Information Assurance focused Organization

Founded in 2005, with head office in Rawalpindi and local offices in Karachi and Lahore, we are Pakistan’s first and only full spectrum Information Assurance focused and specialized company. Named one of the top 100 Tech Innovators in 2018 by TMT Magazine, we offer Services, Solutions, Training, Distribution and R&D epitomized into five dedicated brands: TRIAM, TriSol, TISP, Trisect and Cybersecurity Lab respectively.

Recognized as one of the Top 25 IBM Solution Providers in 2017 by CIO Outlook magazine and Best Service Partner of the year by Kaspersky Lab, we are also a Gold partner of IBM and a value added distributor of Kaspersky Lab. Our continuous R&D, and the resultant international award-winning products brought us honor of being validated and approved by IBM. This distinction along with our in-house training material production and delivery has turned us into an Institute of Excellence of the region in the Information Assurance domain. Read More ..
